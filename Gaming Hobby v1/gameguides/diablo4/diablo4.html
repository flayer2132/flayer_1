Levling Guide

Tips for leveling (1-50)

  • Play on World Tier 1
    • The extra gold earned in WT2 is negligible
    • It's much easier to complete Dungeons and sidequests for Renown in WT1
  • Skip Dungeons & Events
  • Do some Cellars (for crafting resources) & Side Quests
  • Level each region to Renown rank 3 for +5 maximum healing potions and 10 skill points for all characters

What to do at level 50+

  • Complete the World Tier 2 Capstone Dungeon to unlock World Tier 3
  • Obtain Sacred rare and legendary items as these always drop at item power 625+ and are a boost over standard level 50+ rares that only roll up to 624
  • Look for any 700+ items that can be upgraded at the Blacksmith to 725+ (especially weapons for a major damage boost!)
  • Find and imprint optimal legendary affixes to the 625+ gear (rare or legendary) by extracting from found loot or by completing Dungeons and imprinting from codex
  • Look for 625+ items with better affixes and legendary aspects with better rolled stats
  • Level to level 60 to unlock the next tier of Healing Potions and Gems

Items & Item Power

Item Power

All items in Diablo 4 have an 'Item Power' statistic. All stats on items (including damage, affixes etc.) scale with item power. Major increases to stats occur at certain item power thresholds.

An item can be bumped into the next tier by upgrading at the Blacksmith. Eg: a level 700 item can be improved into tier 6 by upgrading it 5 times (+25 item power). Making the total item power 725. For example: upgrading gear from 458 to 461 would be a much higher boost than upgrading 455 to 458.

Note: 'Armour' and 'Damage Per Second' values scale linearly with Item Power and do not benefit from IP breakpoints

Item Power Breakpoints

Tier Item Power Range
I 1-149
II 150-339
III 340-459
IV 460-624
V 625-724
VI 725+

Imprinting

  • Imprinting Legendary Affixes from the Codex always rolls at the lowest value.
  • Legendary aspects can be replaced with other aspects, but not the same aspect.
  • % based aspects don’t scale with item power – so level extracting a level 20 percent-based aspect is just as good as a level 50 one
  • # based aspects scale with item power
  • 2-handed items gain a 100% increase to stat scaling, while amulets gain a 50% increase!

NOTE: Extracted aspects ‘remember’ the item level of the item that they were extracted from. Imprinting an aspect extracted from a level 70 item will change the target item to level 70.

Check the item level on the extracted aspect before imprinting!
